Be Always Curious...

When I started fitness, that was my passion — but it changed as time went on.

I first discovered martial arts through my brother, since he used to train Taekwondo.

What I love about martial arts is the art itself — you can learn new skills, create your own, and it’s such a versatile sport.

Then I moved on to boxing. I learned it on my own, but I felt like it wasn’t fully my thing.

So I tried wrestling — and that’s where it clicked. I really liked it, and my background in weightlifting helped a lot.

I also love MMA, because mixing kicks, punches, and takedowns just feels natural.

Along the journey, I started recording myself and editing my videos on CapCut.

Later, I saw my brother coding, and that got me curious. I started learning HTML and now I’m learning CSS.

I also got interested in biological health after watching a video about it — it seemed fascinating and I want to learn more, because it can really help my future.
